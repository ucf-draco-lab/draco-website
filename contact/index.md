## Lab PI

**Dr. Mike Borowczak** — Associate Professor of Electrical and Computer Engineering, UCF. IEEE and ACM Senior Member. Former hardware security architect (Intel, Texas Instruments). Research funded by NSF, NSA, DOE, Idaho National Laboratory, AMD, Northrop Grumman, and others (~$8.5M since 2018).
